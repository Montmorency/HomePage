H. Lambert

I am a Post-Doctoral Research Assistant at King's College London. My current research focuses on fracture processes in hydrogen embrittled steels . In particular we are focusing on hydrogen occlusion and the effects of strain on grain boundaries in iron and steel. Previously I worked on the optical properties of materials in Feliciano Giustino's group .

My programming interests include studying the properties of materials from first principles. The SternheimerGW and Imeall Grain Boundary Database are two of the projects I have worked on.
